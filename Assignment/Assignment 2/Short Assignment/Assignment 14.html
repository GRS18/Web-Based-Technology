Mahabharata is a major Indian epic which comes under the category of history of Smriti . It is also called Bharat . This epic is a unique religious , mythological , historical and philosophical text of India .This world's longest literary text and epic is one of the main texts of Hinduism . This text is considered the fifth Veda in Hinduism. Although it is considered one of the most unique works of literature , even today this text is an exemplary source for every Indian .
