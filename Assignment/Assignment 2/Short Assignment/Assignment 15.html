This world's longest literary text and epic is one of the main texts of Hinduism . This text is considered the fifth Veda in Hinduism. Although it is considered one of the most unique works of literature , even today this text is an exemplary source for every Indian .



This work is a saga of the history of ancient India . The holiest text of Hinduism, Bhagavad Gita, is contained in it. The entire Mahabharata has about 1,10,000 verses , which is ten times more in size than the Greek poems Iliad and Odyssey .
